AUDEN SHIELD — RITUAL CARTOGRAPHY
After James W. Carey · "Communication is a symbolic process whereby reality is produced, maintained, repaired, and transformed"
FP1: Texts are ceremonies: reading is attending a mass.
FP2: The shield is not an object; it is a staging device for communion.
FP3: A map is not a copy; it configures experience. The map changes the world it "describes."
FP4: What Thetis expected vs. what Hephaestos made = the ritual break that transforms the reader.
